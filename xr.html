Experience Mars Perseverance in AR

Wanting to learn more about the Mars Perserverance Rover and to better understand how the use of AR and VR can be used for people to better space technologies. I'm looking to learn and building fun/interactive material for people and start more engagment with STEM. This will get updated as the AR/VR scenes progress.

AR will only work on your mobile device. So unfortunately I haven't been able to test this on Andriod yet but it should just workin in any browser. For Iphone, please install the XR Viewer app on your Iphone. Click the hyper-link and explore the rover in AR. If an issue occurs please just refresh the webpage. Sorry for the inconvience!
